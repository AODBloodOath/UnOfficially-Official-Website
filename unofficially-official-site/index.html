What it is

Unofficially Official is designed to feel like vanilla DayZ: clean loot balance, high-risk decisions, and survival-first pacing. We add a handful of tasteful POIs and locked keycard rooms to create extra goals without turning the server into a modded arcade.

What it isn’t

No boosted loot madness. No “pay to win” gear. No convoluted rulebook. If you can survive on official, you’ll feel right at home here.

Features
